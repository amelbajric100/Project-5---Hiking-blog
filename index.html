About Us

Description

Our team consists of 3 experienced mountaineer: Mufid, Suad and Amel. They will provide a great mountain tour for you and for your company. You will have opportuinty to discover beauttiful nature of our mountains in BiH.

History of hiking club

Our hiking club Bjelašnica was founded in 2015. We had a vision to provide mountain tour for tourist and for everyone who wants to discover mountains and doesn't have much experience. We realized many tours.

What we offer

If you want to join our team and discover any mountain in BiH, feel free to contact us. We offer 1 day our or 2 days tour for you and you company.

Location of hiking club

We are located in Hadžići - municipality near Sarajevo. Contact number: +38733 418 003 & +38762 199 199

Most popular tours

Most popular tours are: Bjelašnica - Opservatorij (2067m) Bjelašnica - Umoljani - Lukomir (old Bosnian villages) Vlašić - Travnik (1967m) Maglić - the highest mountain in Bosnia (2386m)

Prices

One-day tour: 200 KM per person. Two-days tour: 300 KM per person. Price depends of the location of choosen mountain. If you choose mountain near Sarajevo than you don't need to pay for transport. If we go outside of Sarajevo Canton you are supposed to pay transport.
